    From September 2010 to June 2014, I studied at the School of Information Science and Engineering, Luoyang Institute of Science and Technology, majoring in Computer Science and Technology, and obtained a Bachelor's Degree. From September 2014 to June 2017, I pursued further studies in the major of Computer Science and Technology at Liaoning Normal University, earning a Master's Degree. Subsequently, from September 2017 to June 2021, I pursued a Doctoral Degree in Computer Applications and Software at Dalian University of Technology.In July 2021, I joined the School of Information Science and Engineering, Henan University of Technology, starting as an Instructor. With a rigorous attitude in teaching and outstanding achievements in scientific research, after more than three years of accumulation and growth, I was successfully promoted to Associate Professor in January 2025. This clear career advancement path fully demonstrates my capabilities and potential in the professional field.In terms of research directions, I have long focused on three core areas: hyperspectral image classification, granular computing, and manifold learning. Through in-depth exploration, I have achieved fruitful academic results. Up to now, I have published a number of important papers on high-level domestic and international academic platforms. Among them, many papers have been published in CAS (Chinese Academy of Sciences) Tier-1 journals, such as IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, Applied Soft Computing, Expert Systems with Applications, and Information Sciences, with some papers in the minor revision stage or under submission. Additionally, several other achievements have been published in CAS Tier-2 journals like Neurocomputing, and I have also published an academic paper at IJCAI 2019, a CCFA-ranked conference, gradually expanding my academic influence.Furthermore, I preside over the National Natural Science Foundation of China (Youth Project) titled "Research on Text Representation Models Integrating Manifold Structure," which runs from January 2023 to December 2025. This experience of leading the project not only showcases my independent scientific research capabilities but also reflects the recognition of my research direction and academic level by relevant fields, providing valuable research results and insights for the development of areas such as hyperspectral image analysis and manifold learning.
