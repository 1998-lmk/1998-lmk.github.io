    Yonghe Chu 楚永贺

    School of Information Science and Engineering, Henan University of Technology

    • Zhengzhou, China
    • dblp

    Chu Yonghe, male, holds a Doctor of Engineering degree and is an associate professor and master's supervisor. He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree from Luoyang Institute of Science and Technology in 2014, his Master of Engineering degree from Liaoning Normal University in 2017, and his Doctor of Engineering degree from Dalian University of Technology in 2021. In July 2021, he joined the School of Information Science and Engineering at Henan University of Technology, where he was successively appointed as a lecturer. From June 2023 to present, he has been a postdoctoral fellow at Nantong University, under the supervision of Professor Ding Weiping. He was promoted to associate professor in January 2025. His research mainly focuses on hyperspectral image classification, granular computing, and manifold learning. In the past five years, he has led a National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC) Youth Project titled "Research on Text Representation Model Integrating Manifold Structure". He has published numerous papers in high-level academic platforms both domestically and internationally, many of which have been featured in top-tier journals of the Chinese Academy of Sciences, including IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systems, IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, IEEE Transactions on Circuits and Systems for Video Technology, Applied Soft Computing, Expert Systems with Applications, and Information Sciences.
