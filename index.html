    • dblp

    Dr. Yonghe Chu is an Associate Professor and Master’s Supervisor at Henan University of Technology. He received his Bachelor of Engineering degree from Luoyang Institute of Science and Technology in 2014, his Master’s degree from Liaoning Normal University in 2017, and his Ph.D. in Engineering from Dalian University of Technology in 2021. He conducted postdoctoral research at Nantong University under the supervision of Professor Weiping Ding. Dr. Chu began his academic career at Henan University of Technology as a Lecturer in July 2021 and was promoted to Associate Professor in January 2025. His research focuses on hyperspectral image classification, granular computing, and manifold learning. In terms of research achievements, over the past five years, he has presided over the National Natural Science Foundation of China Youth Program project titled "Research on Text Representation Models Integrating Manifold Structures." He has also published several academic papers in top-tier journals, including IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks and Learning Systemsand IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing, which are classified as Tier 1 journals in the Chinese Academy of Sciences ranking.
